How DecisionTwin AI works

Explainable by design. Every lost deal has a reason you can act on.

1

Buying committee simulation

Every B2B deal is decided by a committee, not one buyer. We sample synthetic buyers from five real-world roles, each with its own decision drivers:

Procurement Manager30%Price, payment terms
Technical Buyer20%Delivery, spec fit
Business Decision Maker20%Speed, urgency
End User20%Delivery, loyalty
CFO10%Price, risk
2

4-stage explainable decision journey

RFQ Submission→Quote Evaluation→Negotiation→Approval→Purchase

At each stage a buyer may drop — and we record why: won't engage, quote too slow, price gap, delivery, payment terms, or internal veto. No black-box scores.

3

The Revenue Leak Map

We aggregate every lost deal, group by stage and reason, multiply by deal value, and sort by £ impact. The top row is your highest-ROI fix — scaled to your annual pipeline.

4

Compare before you commit

Run any two scenarios side by side and ship the variant that wins. Test the price rise, the faster quote, the expedited delivery — without risking a single real customer.
